Experience the Ultimate Southern California Lifestyle!  Malibu Valley Estates is presented by Master Developer Malibu Valley Partners.

This exclusive enclave of 26 homesites is generously situated on over approximately 100 acres allowing each custom family estate privacy, elegance and unobstructed panoramic views of the Santa Monica Mountains.

The Craftsman is perched atop one of the most elevated homesites in this gated community and will be ready for occupancy in Spring, 2021.  This masterpiece boasts 8000 square feet of total living space (approx. 6400 square feet interior) with a soaring curved staircase and showcases a resort-style infinity pool and spa with outside shower and bathroom/changing room.

At last, a home that has it all: impeccably designed floorplan with paramount finishes, featuring 5 large bedroom suites, plus an additional office/media room.  The master suite reveals breathtaking views, a makeup vanity filled with natural light, separate sinks and Toto washlets, accompanied by a soaking tub and oversized shower with dual shower heads.

Enjoy the incredible sunset views from the home’s expansive balconies and porches, along with the ambiance of its outdoor natural gas lanterns and fireplaces.

The kitchen is exquisitely crafted with high-end Gaggenau appliances, including a plumbed coffee machine.

There is also an 18’ wide bifold glass wall system for seamless indoor/outdoor living and dining atop one of the area’s most expansive views.

This well thought out home has direct access to the pantry from its 4-car garage, as well as family and laundry rooms on each floor.

The Craftsman is a smart home with wireless Crestron system allowing automated control from anywhere at anytime with the touch of a button or smart phone.

Situated on 2.3 acres, this privately gated property overlooks historic King Gillette Ranch and Malibu Creek State Park, which encompass 9,000 combined acres for hiking, mountain biking and horseback riding.

Offered at $6,495,000
